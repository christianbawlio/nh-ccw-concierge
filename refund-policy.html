Application Concierge Service ($95)

Because our concierge service involves the immediate preparation and processing of your application forms, refunds are handled as follows:

  • Before form preparation begins: If you request a refund before we begin working on your application forms, you are entitled to a full refund of $95.

Defense Training Course Subscription ($35/month)

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at support@ccwconcierge.com with the following information:

Refund requests are processed within 5-7 business days. Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method.
